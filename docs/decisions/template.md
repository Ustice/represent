# ADR NNNN: Decision title

- Status: proposed | accepted | blocked | superseded
- Date: YYYY-MM-DD
- Objective: issue or `None — reason`
- Owning issue: issue or `None — reason`

## Context

State the bounded decision and the evidence that makes it necessary.

## Decision

Record the decision. A blocked ADR states the smallest decision required to
resume.

## Alternatives considered

List credible alternatives and why each was not selected. Preserve unresolved
disagreement explicitly.

## Consequences

State guarantees, risks, follow-up work, and compatibility effects.

## Review evidence

- Reviewer: person or independent agent role
- Outcome: accepted | changes requested | blocked
- Evidence reviewed: links to clauses, tests, drills, or checklists
- Unresolved disagreements: links or `None — reason`
- Accepted on: YYYY-MM-DD or `Not accepted`
